<p><p>Power Integrated Modules (PIMs) encompass various technologies, primarily focusing on IGBT (Insulated Gate Bipolar Transistor) and MOSFET (Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor Field-Effect Transistor) markets. IGBTs are commonly used for high-voltage applications due to their efficiency and high switching speed, making them ideal for industrial and automotive sectors. Conversely, MOSFETs excel in low-voltage applications, offering fast switching and low power loss, suitable for consumer electronics and renewable energy systems. Both technologies contribute to enhancing energy efficiency in power electronics applications.</p></p>
