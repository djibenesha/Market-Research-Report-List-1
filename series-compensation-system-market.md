<p><p>Series compensation systems are categorized into three market types based on voltage levels: low voltage, medium voltage, and high voltage. Low voltage systems typically operate below 1 kV, suitable for residential and light commercial applications. Medium voltage systems, ranging from 1 kV to 35 kV, are often used in industrial and utility settings for improved efficiency. High voltage systems, exceeding 35 kV, are crucial for long-distance power transmission, enhancing system stability and reducing losses in large-scale electrical networks.</p></p>
