<p><p>The Industrial Gas Phase Filtration System market comprises three primary types: Activated Carbon, Potassium Permanganate, and Blends. Potassium Permanganate is utilized for its oxidizing properties, efficiently removing certain contaminants, especially hydrogen sulfide and mercaptans. Blends combine multiple filtration materials to enhance performance and tackle a broader range of pollutants. Each type serves critical roles in maintaining air quality and compliance within industrial settings.</p></p>
