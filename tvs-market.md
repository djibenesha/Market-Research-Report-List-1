<p><strong>The TVS Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Uni-polar TVS</li><li>Bi-polar TVS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The TVS market can be categorized into uni-polar and bi-polar types based on the number of voltage connections. Uni-polar TVS devices protect against voltage transients in single-direction applications, typically utilized in positive voltage systems. In contrast, bi-polar TVS devices safeguard circuits that can experience voltage in both positive and negative directions, making them suitable for dual-supply systems. Each type is designed to enhance circuit reliability by absorbing unwanted voltage spikes, ensuring effective protection for electronic components.</p></p>
