<p><p>The lubricant viscosity modifier market includes three main types: OCP-based, PMA-based, and styrenics-based modifiers. OCP-based modifiers enhance the low-temperature performance and shear stability of lubricants, improving their efficiency. PMA-based modifiers provide excellent viscosity stability across a wide temperature range, making them suitable for high-performance applications. Styrenics-based modifiers offer versatile performance characteristics by improving oxidative stability and reducing friction. Each type plays a crucial role in optimizing lubricant performance in various industrial and automotive applications.</p></p>
