<p><ul><li>Single-Acting</li><li>Quarter-Turn Actuator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pneumatic actuator market includes two primary types: single-acting and quarter-turn actuators. Single-acting actuators use pneumatic pressure to move in one direction, relying on a spring return for the opposite movement, making them efficient for applications needing simple linear motion. In contrast, quarter-turn actuators are designed to rotate a valve or damper a quarter turn, enabling precise control of fluid flow. Both types are essential in various industrial automation and control applications, contributing to improved efficiency and safety.</p></p>
