Glass wool is produced from recycled glass fibers, offering excellent sound and thermal insulation in buildings. Cellular glass, made from crushed glass and a foaming agent, provides rigid insulation with high compressive strength and moisture resistance, making it ideal for industrial applications. Each type caters to specific insulation needs.</p></p>
