<p><strong>The Deflectometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Falling Weight Deflectometer</li><li>Light Weight Deflectometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The deflectometer market comprises two main types: Falling Weight Deflectometers (FWD) and Light Weight Deflectometers (LWD). FWDs utilize a controlled drop weight to assess the load-bearing capacity of pavements, providing detailed insights into structural integrity. In contrast, LWDs are designed for rapid, lightweight testing, making them ideal for field applications where portability and ease of use are crucial. Both types are essential for evaluating road conditions and supporting effective maintenance strategies in civil engineering.</p></p>
