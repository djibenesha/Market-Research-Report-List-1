<p><strong>The Automotive Body Sealing System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>EPDM</li><li>TPE</li><li>PVC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Body Sealing System market comprises three main types: EPDM (Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer), TPE (Thermoplastic Elastomer), and PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride). EPDM is favored for its excellent weather resistance and durability, making it ideal for sealing windows and doors. TPE combines the benefits of rubber and plastic, offering flexibility and recyclability, suitable for various sealing applications. PVC, known for its affordability and versatility, is used in budget-friendly vehicles but may lack the longevity of EPDM and TPE.</p></p>
