<p><p>Missile warning systems market encompasses various types that utilize different detection technologies. Infrared-based systems track heat signatures from missiles, providing real-time alerts. Pulse Doppler-based systems leverage radar technology to identify and track moving objects by analyzing their Doppler shift, enhancing target discrimination. Ultraviolet-based systems detect the unique UV radiation emitted during missile launches, offering another layer of surveillance. Each technology plays a crucial role in enhancing defense capabilities against missile threats and ensuring timely response strategies.</p></p>
