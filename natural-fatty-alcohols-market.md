<p><p>The natural fatty alcohols market is primarily categorized into saturated and unsaturated alcohols. Saturated alcohols, derived from sources like palm and coconut oil, are fully hydrogenated and possess a straight-chain structure, making them ideal for applications in cosmetics, detergents, and emulsifiers. Unsaturated alcohols, on the other hand, contain one or more double bonds, which can enhance their reactivity and functional properties, making them suitable for specialized applications in the chemical and cosmetic industries. Both types play crucial roles in various formulations.</p></p>
