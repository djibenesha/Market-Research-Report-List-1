<p><strong>The Board Terminal Block Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Through Hole</li><li>Surface Mount</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The board terminal block market comprises two primary types: through-hole and surface mount. Through-hole terminal blocks are designed for insertion into pre-drilled holes in a PCB, providing robust mechanical support and reliable connections, suitable for high-power applications. Surface mount terminal blocks, on the other hand, are mounted directly onto the surface of the PCB, facilitating automated assembly and saving space. Both types cater to diverse applications in industries like automotive, telecommunications, and industrial electronics, enhancing connectivity and performance.</p></p>
