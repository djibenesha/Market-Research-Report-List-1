<p><p>The wind turbine fiber market primarily consists of two types: glass fiber and carbon fiber. In contrast, carbon fiber, though more expensive, offers superior strength-to-weight ratio, enhancing performance and durability. The choice between these materials depends on factors like turbine size, design requirements, and production costs, influencing overall efficiency and longevity of wind energy systems.</p></p>
