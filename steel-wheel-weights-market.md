<p><ul><li>Clip On Wheel Weights</li><li>Adhesive Wheel Weights</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steel wheel weights market comprises two primary types: clip-on wheel weights and adhesive wheel weights. Clip-on weights are designed to attach securely to the rim of the wheel, offering easy installation and removal, making them popular for service shop applications. Adhesive wheel weights, on the other hand, utilize a self-adhesive backing to stick to the inner surface of the wheel, providing a more streamlined appearance and reducing the risk of damage during installation. Both types cater to diverse automotive balancing needs.</p></p>
