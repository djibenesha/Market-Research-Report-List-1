<p><p>The iron and steel slag market encompasses various processing methods. The hot method involves the immediate processing of molten slag, ensuring optimal recovery of metals. The hot boring method involves extracting slag from the furnace while it's still hot, enhancing efficiency. The roller method utilizes hydraulic rollers to crush and reshape slag for specific applications. Other methods may include mechanical granulation and air cooling techniques. Each method serves distinct purposes in recycling and repurposing slag for construction and industrial uses.</p></p>
