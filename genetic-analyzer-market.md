<p><p>The genetic analyzer market encompasses various technologies for analyzing genetic material. DNA microarrays allow simultaneous examination of thousands of genes, PCR (Polymerase Chain Reaction) amplifies specific DNA sequences for better analysis. Next Generation Sequencing (NGS) enables rapid and cost-effective sequencing of entire genomes. Serial Analysis of Gene Expression (SAGE) quantifies gene expression levels, while Northern blotting is used to detect specific RNA sequences. Each method plays a crucial role in genomic research, diagnostics, and personalized medicine.</p></p>
