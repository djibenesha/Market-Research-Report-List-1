<p><p>The Hollow Polysulfone Dialyzer market is divided into two main types: Low Flux Membrane and High Flux Membrane dialyzers. Low Flux Membrane dialyzers are designed for patients who require less aggressive treatment and offer selective removal of smaller solutes, providing enhanced blood compatibility. In contrast, High Flux Membrane dialyzers allow for the efficient clearance of larger molecules and solutes, making them suitable for patients requiring more intensive dialysis therapy. Both types cater to varying patient needs within renal care.</p></p>
