<p><ul><li>Single-path Interferometer</li><li>Multipath Interferometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor white light interferometer market is categorized into two main types: single-path interferometers and multipath interferometers. Single-path interferometers utilize a single optical path for measuring interference patterns, typically leading to simpler configurations and applications in precise thickness measurements. In contrast, multipath interferometers employ multiple optical paths, enhancing sensitivity and enabling complex measurements across various surfaces and materials, often used in advanced metrology and research applications. Together, these types cater to diverse industrial and scientific needs.</p></p>
