Umbilicals are bundles of tubes and cables that supply power, control signals, and chemicals to subsea equipment. Risers connect the seabed to surface facilities, facilitating the transportation of oil and gas. Flowlines enable the movement of produced fluids from subsea wells to processing platforms. This market plays a pivotal role in enhancing subsea production efficiency and safety.</p></p>
