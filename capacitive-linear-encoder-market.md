Axle type encoders are designed for applications where movement is based on rotating or linear axles, providing precise position measurements. In contrast, shaft type encoders are focused on measuring the position of rotating shafts, often used in machinery and automation systems. Both types leverage capacitive sensing technology for enhanced resolution and accuracy, making them suitable for diverse industrial and engineering applications.</p></p>
