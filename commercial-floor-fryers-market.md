<p><p>The commercial floor fryers market is primarily divided into two types: gas commercial floor fryers and electric commercial floor fryers. Gas fryers utilize natural gas or propane, offering high heating efficiency and quicker recovery times, making them ideal for high-volume cooking. In contrast, electric fryers use electric heating elements, providing consistent temperature control and easier installation, often preferred in smaller kitchens. Both types cater to different restaurant needs, reflecting varying operational preferences and energy availabilities.</p></p>
