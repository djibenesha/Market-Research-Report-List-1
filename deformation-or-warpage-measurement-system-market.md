<p><ul><li>Contact-Type</li><li>Contactless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Deformation or Warpage Measurement System Market is categorized into two main types: contact-type and contactless systems. Contact-type systems involve physical contact with the object, utilizing probes or sensors to measure deformation directly, ensuring high accuracy but potentially causing surface damage. In contrast, contactless systems employ technologies such as laser or optical methods to measure warpage remotely, allowing for safe assessments without influencing the object's integrity. Both types cater to diverse applications in manufacturing, aerospace, and quality control.</p></p>
