<p><ul><li>Gate Valve</li><li>Globe Valve</li><li>Butterfly Valve</li><li>Ball Valve</li><li>Check Valve</li><li>Diaphragm Valve</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nuclear valves market encompasses various types designed for specific applications within nuclear plants. Gate valves allow or block flow with minimal pressure drop. Globe valves are used for throttling and flow regulation. Butterfly valves provide quick shut-off with a quarter-turn operation. Ball valves offer reliable sealing and fast operation. Check valves prevent backflow, while diaphragm valves ensure tight sealing for corrosive fluids. Other valves may include safety and control types, catering to diverse operational requirements in nuclear facilities.</p></p>
