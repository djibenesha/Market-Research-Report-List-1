<p><ul><li>Two-Way Converters</li><li>Three-Way Converters</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive catalytic converter market is primarily divided into three types: Two-Way Converters, Three-Way Converters, and Other converters. Two-Way Converters focus on converting carbon monoxide and unburned hydrocarbons into less harmful emissions. Three-Way Converters are more advanced, addressing nitrogen oxides alongside the two pollutants handled by Two-Way types, thus significantly reducing overall emissions. Other converters may include different designs or technologies, such as diesel oxidation catalysts and selective catalytic reduction systems, catering to specific vehicle needs and regulatory standards.</p></p>
