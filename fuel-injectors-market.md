<p><strong>The Fuel Injectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Throttle Body Injection</li><li>Multiport Injection</li><li>Direct Injection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fuel injectors market comprises three primary types: Throttle Body Injection (TBI), Multiport Fuel Injection (MPFI), and Direct Injection (DI). TBI centralizes fuel delivery at the throttle body, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness. MPFI distributes fuel to each cylinder, enhancing efficiency and performance by promoting better atomization. DI injects fuel directly into the combustion chamber, maximizing power and fuel efficiency while reducing emissions. Each type plays a vital role in optimizing engine performance and adapting to evolving automotive technologies.</p></p>
