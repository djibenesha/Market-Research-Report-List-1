<p><p>Auto dialer systems are categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based auto dialers operate over the internet, offering flexibility and scalability without the need for extensive hardware. They enable easy access and often include features like real-time monitoring and analytics. In contrast, on-premises auto dialers are installed locally within an organization’s infrastructure, providing greater control and customization. However, they often require significant investment in hardware and maintenance, making them suitable for businesses with specific compliance needs.</p></p>
