<p><p>The absorbable hemostatic sponge market is segmented into two main types: skin gelatin and bone gelatin. Skin gelatin sponges are derived from the collagen found in animal skin and are designed for topical applications to control bleeding during surgical procedures. Bone gelatin sponges, on the other hand, are sourced from collagen in bone tissue and are often used in orthopedics and dental surgeries. Both types facilitate clot formation and promote wound healing while being absorbed by the body over time.</p></p>
