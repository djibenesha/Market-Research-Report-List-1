<p><strong>The Lubricant for Metal Forming Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tube Drawing Lubricants</li><li>Hot Forming Lubricants</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lubricant market for metal forming consists of two primary types: tube drawing lubricants and hot forming lubricants. Tube drawing lubricants are specifically designed for the drawing process of tubes, providing reduced friction and improved surface finish during elongation. Hot forming lubricants, on the other hand, are used in high-temperature processes, such as forging or stamping, to prevent metal adhesion and minimize tool wear. Both types are essential for enhancing efficiency, preserving tool life, and ensuring high-quality metal products.</p></p>
