<p><p>The PS resin market is divided primarily into General Purpose Polystyrene (GPPS) and High Impact Polystyrene (HIPS). GPPS is characterized by its clarity and rigidity, making it ideal for applications like packaging and consumer goods. In contrast, HIPS is modified with rubber, enhancing its impact resistance and durability, which is suitable for products requiring strength, such as household appliances and automotive components. Both types cater to diverse industries, addressing specific performance needs and applications.</p></p>
