Electric Heating Film consists of thin, flexible film elements that evenly distribute heat and are easy to install. Carbon Crystal systems utilize carbon technology for efficient, radiant heat distribution and are known for their environmental benefits. Each type offers distinct advantages, catering to diverse flooring applications and consumer preferences.</p></p>
