<p><strong>The Pneumatic Rolling Motor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Torque Controlled</li><li>Stall Torque</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pneumatic rolling motor market is segmented into two main types: torque controlled and stall torque motors. Torque-controlled motors regulate output torque to maintain consistent performance, making them ideal for applications requiring precise control and smooth operation. Stall torque motors, on the other hand, deliver maximum torque at standstill, providing high initial power for heavy-duty tasks. Both types cater to different operational needs, with torque-controlled motors emphasizing precision and efficiency, while stall torque motors focus on delivering peak performance in challenging conditions.</p></p>
