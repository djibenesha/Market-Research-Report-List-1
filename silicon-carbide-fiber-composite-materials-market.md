<p><ul><li>Metal-based</li><li>Ceramic-based</li><li>Polymer-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Silicon carbide fiber composite materials are categorized into three main types: metal-based, ceramic-based, and polymer-based composites. Metal-based composites incorporate silicon carbide fibers within metal matrices, enhancing strength and thermal stability for applications in aerospace and automotive sectors. Ceramic-based composites utilize silicon carbide fibers embedded in ceramic matrices, offering superior heat resistance and wear properties, ideal for high-temperature environments. Polymer-based composites combine silicon carbide fibers with polymer resins, providing lightweight and flexible options for industries requiring corrosion resistance and improved mechanical performance.</p></p>
