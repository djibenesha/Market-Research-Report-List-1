<p><strong>The Remote Monitoring Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>External</li><li>Implantable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The remote monitoring equipment market comprises two primary types: external and implantable devices. External devices, such as wearable gadgets or sensors, monitor health parameters like heart rate and blood pressure without invasive procedures. They are user-friendly and often connected to smartphones for easy data access. In contrast, implantable devices are surgically placed within the body to provide continuous monitoring of critical metrics, such as glucose levels or cardiac activity. Both types enhance patient care and enable timely medical interventions.</p></p>
