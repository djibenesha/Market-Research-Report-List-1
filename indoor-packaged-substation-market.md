Liquid-Filled Transformers use oil to dissipate heat and provide cooling, offering high efficiency and reliability in larger installations. Dry Type Transformers, on the other hand, utilize insulation materials to eliminate the need for liquid cooling, making them safer and more suitable for indoor applications with limited space. Both types cater to different operational needs while ensuring effective power distribution in urban settings.</p></p>
