<p><p>Pharma Grade PLA (Polylactic Acid) is categorized into three types based on its chemical composition and properties. </p><p>D Type PLA consists solely of D-lactic acid and is valued for its high crystallinity and mechanical strength, suitable for specialty drug delivery systems. </p><p>L Type PLA, made from L-lactic acid, offers good biodegradability and is commonly used in medical applications. </p><p>DL Type PLA, a copolymer of D and L-lactic acids, merges properties from both, providing versatility and enhanced performance in various pharmaceutical applications.</p></p>
