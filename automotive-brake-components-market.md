<p><p>The automotive brake components market can be categorized into three main types: drum brakes, disc brakes, and other brake systems. </p><p>Drum brakes utilize a cylindrical drum that rotates with the wheel, employing shoes that press against the inner surface to create friction. </p><p>Disc brakes feature a flat disc, with calipers that clamp brake pads against the disc surface, offering superior heat dissipation and performance. </p><p>Other brake types may include regenerative braking systems and air brakes, catering to specialized vehicles or applications.</p></p>
