<p><p>The organic filler market is categorized into thermoset and thermoplastic types. Thermoset fillers are used in applications where rigidity and heat resistance are crucial, as they harden permanently when cured, providing enhanced structural integrity. In contrast, thermoplastic fillers remain malleable upon heating, allowing for easier processing and recycling. This flexibility makes thermoplastics suitable for applications that require reshaping. Both types are essential for improving material properties in various industries, including construction, automotive, and consumer goods.</p></p>
