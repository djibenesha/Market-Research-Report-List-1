<p><ul><li>Buck Driver</li><li>Boost Driver</li><li>Multi-channel Driver</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Low Voltage AC-DC LED Driver market comprises three main types: Buck Drivers, Boost Drivers, and Multi-channel Drivers. Buck Drivers efficiently step down voltage to power LEDs, ideal for applications with a higher voltage input. Boost Drivers, on the other hand, increase voltage to accommodate LEDs requiring more power. Multi-channel Drivers manage multiple LED outputs, allowing for versatile control and dimming across different channels. Together, these drivers cater to diverse lighting solutions, enhancing energy efficiency and performance in LED applications.</p></p>
