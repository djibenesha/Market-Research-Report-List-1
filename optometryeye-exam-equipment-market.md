<p><p>The optometry/eye exam equipment market includes various technologies essential for ocular assessment. Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT) provides detailed cross-sectional images of the retina. Fundus cameras capture images of the interior surface of the eye. Perimeters measure visual fields, while topography maps the cornea's curvature. Lensmeters assess prescription lenses, and chart projectors display visual acuity tests. Wavefront analyzers evaluate the eye's optical system, aiding in customizing vision correction, thereby enhancing patient care and diagnostics in eye health.</p></p>
