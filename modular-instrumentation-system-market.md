<p><p>The Modular Instrumentation System market comprises three primary platforms: PXI, AXIe, and VXI. The PXI platform, renowned for its compactness and high-speed data transfer, is commonly used in automated testing and measurement applications. AXIe offers scalability and enhanced performance for high-bandwidth applications, making it ideal for advanced testing environments. Meanwhile, the VXI platform, designed for legacy systems, supports modular instrumentation in larger setups, focusing on reliability and integration with existing hardware. Each platform serves distinct application needs within the market.</p></p>
