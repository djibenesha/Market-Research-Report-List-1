<p><p>A bilgewater separator is a crucial piece of equipment used in marine vessels to treat bilge water before it is discharged into the ocean. These separators utilize various technologies, such as coalescence and filtration, to remove oil and solids from the water, ensuring compliance with international maritime standards.</p><p>The bilgewater separator market is experiencing significant growth due to increasing maritime trade, heightened environmental regulations, and an emphasis on sustainability in shipping operations.
