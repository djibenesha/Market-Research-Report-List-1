<p><ul><li>Hydrocyclone</li><li>Gas Cyclone</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cyclone separation markets consist of two primary types: hydrocyclones and gas cyclones. Hydrocyclones utilize centrifugal forces to separate solids from liquids in applications like mineral processing and wastewater treatment, effectively enhancing efficiency in liquid-solid separation. Gas cyclones, on the other hand, are designed to separate particulate matter from gas streams, primarily used in industrial processes such as combustion and air pollution control. Both types leverage centrifugal action to achieve effective separation, catering to different sectors and operational needs.</p></p>
