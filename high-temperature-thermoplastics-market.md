<p><p>High-temperature thermoplastics encompass a range of specialized materials designed to withstand extreme conditions. High Temperature Fluoropolymers (FPs) offer chemical resistance and thermal stability. High-Performance Polyamide (HPPA) provides robustness and versatility for demanding applications. Polyphenylene Sulfide (PPS) features excellent dimensional stability. Sulfone Polymers (SP) exhibit high-temperature resistance and durability. Liquid Crystal Polymers (LCP) are noted for their high strength and low thermal expansion. Aromatic Ketone Polymers (AKP) combine thermal stability with mechanical performance, while Polyimide (PI) is renowned for its exceptional heat resistance and electrical insulation properties.</p></p>
