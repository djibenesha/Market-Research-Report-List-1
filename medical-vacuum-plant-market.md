<p><p>The medical vacuum plant market consists of two main configurations: single pump and multi-pump setups. Single pump configurations utilize one vacuum pump to generate suction, suitable for smaller facilities with less demand. In contrast, multi-pump configurations involve multiple pumps working in tandem, providing consistent and reliable vacuum for larger hospitals or surgical centers with higher volume needs. The choice between these configurations depends on the specific requirements for efficiency, reliability, and space availability in healthcare settings.</p></p>
