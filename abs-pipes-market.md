<p><p>The ABS pipes market includes various types designed for specific applications. Two-way pipes are straight conduits used for direct fluid or gas flow. Tee pipes feature a T-shaped junction, allowing flow diversion in three directions, ideal for branching systems. Cross pipes have four openings, enabling connections in multiple directions, suitable for complex networks. Other market types include elbows, reducers, and adapters, catering to diverse plumbing and industrial needs. Each type ensures efficient flow management and system flexibility.</p></p>
