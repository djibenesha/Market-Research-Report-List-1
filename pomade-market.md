<p><p>The pomade market is categorized into three main types: water-based, oil-based, and concrete class pomades. Water-based pomade offers easy washability and provides a medium hold with a natural finish, making it popular for daily use. Oil-based pomade delivers a strong hold and shines but can be harder to wash out. Concrete class pomade is known for its extreme hold, typically used for intricate styles and hair that requires maximum control. Each type caters to different styling needs and preferences.</p></p>
