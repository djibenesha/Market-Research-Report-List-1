<p><p>Brewery management software is categorized into two main types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based solutions offer the advantage of accessibility from any internet-enabled device, promoting collaboration and real-time data updates. They typically have lower upfront costs and facilitate easier scalability. In contrast, on-premises software is installed locally on a brewery's infrastructure, providing greater control and data security. However, it often requires higher initial investment and ongoing maintenance, making it suitable for larger operations with specific IT needs.</p></p>
