<p><p>ESC (Electronic Speed Controller) market divides primarily into two types: brushed and brushless ESCs. Brushed ESCs are designed for use with brushed motors, providing simpler control and often lower costs, making them suitable for hobbyists and basic applications. In contrast, brushless ESCs are tailored for brushless motors, offering higher efficiency, better performance, and increased lifespan, making them ideal for advanced applications such as drones and high-performance RC vehicles. The growth of electric vehicles boosts demand for both market segments.</p></p>
