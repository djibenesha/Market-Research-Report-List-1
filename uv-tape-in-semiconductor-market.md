<p><p>UV tape in the semiconductor market is categorized into three main substrate types: PO (Polyolefin) substrate, PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate) substrate, and PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) substrate. PO substrate is known for its excellent adhesion and thermal resistance, making it suitable for diverse applications. PET substrate offers high clarity and durability, ideal for precision tasks. PVC substrate provides cost-effectiveness and versatility, often used in general-purpose applications. Each type serves distinct needs in semiconductor manufacturing and packaging processes.</p></p>
