<p><p>The Cardiac Marker Rapid Test market consists of various types that aid in diagnosing cardiac conditions. Troponin tests are the most specific for myocardial injury, while CK-MB tests measure cardiac muscle damage. Myoglobin tests detect early damage but are less specific. Other markers may include B-type natriuretic peptide (BNP) and C-reactive protein (CRP), which help assess heart failure and inflammation, respectively. This diverse range of tests enables prompt and accurate diagnosis of heart-related issues.</p></p>
