<p><strong>The Radar Level Transmitters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-contact Radar Level Transmitter</li><li>Guided Wave Radar Level Transmitter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The radar level transmitters market is primarily divided into two types: non-contact radar level transmitters and guided wave radar level transmitters. Non-contact radar transmitters use microwave signals that are emitted and reflected off the surface of the material, making them suitable for various applications without any physical contact. In contrast, guided wave radar transmitters utilize a probe that transmits signals along a cable or rod, allowing for precise level measurement in challenging conditions like high pressure or temperature environments.</p></p>
