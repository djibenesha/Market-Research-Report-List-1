<p><p>The carbon materials market consists of several key types: Carbon Nanotubes (CNTs), Carbon Nanofibers (CNFs), and Nano Carbon Balls. Carbon Nanotubes are cylindrical nanostructures known for their exceptional strength and electrical conductivity, making them valuable in electronics and materials science. Carbon Nanofibers are elongated fibers that enhance mechanical properties in composites. Nano Carbon Balls are spherical carbon structures utilized in batteries and as catalysts due to their high surface area and electrical characteristics. Each type has unique applications, driving innovation across industries.</p></p>
