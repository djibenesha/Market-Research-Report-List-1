<p><ul><li>WDM</li><li>DWDM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Optical Transport Network (OTN) market primarily consists of two key types: Wavelength Division Multiplexing (WDM) and Dense Wavelength Division Multiplexing (DWDM). WDM technology allows multiple light wavelengths to carry data over a single optical fiber, enhancing capacity and efficiency. DWDM takes this further by using closely spaced wavelengths, enabling even greater data transmission rates and maximizing fiber utilization. Both technologies are essential for supporting the growing demand for high-speed connectivity in telecommunications and data center environments.</p></p>
