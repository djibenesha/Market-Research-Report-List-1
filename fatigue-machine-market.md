<p><p>The fatigue machine market encompasses various types based on frequency: low, medium, and high frequency. Low frequency fatigue machines operate at lower stress levels, ideal for long-term testing of materials under static and dynamic loads. Medium frequency machines provide a balance, suitable for standard fatigue tests across diverse materials. High frequency fatigue machines are designed for rapid cycling, focusing on high-stress applications, often used in aerospace and automotive testing, where quick insights into material behavior under stress are critical.</p></p>
