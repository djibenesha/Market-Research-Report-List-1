<p><p>Electrostatic Discharge (ESD) foam packaging is essential for protecting electronic components from static electricity. There are three primary types: Metal foam incorporates conductive materials, offering a robust solution for high-risk environments. Conductive foam provides a continuous conductive path, ideal for sensitive devices by preventing charge accumulation. Dissipative polymer foam, on the other hand, safely dissipates static electricity over time, making it suitable for everyday handling of electronics. Each type caters to different ESD protection needs in the market.</p></p>
