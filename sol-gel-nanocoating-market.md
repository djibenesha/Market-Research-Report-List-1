<p><p>The Sol-Gel Nanocoating market can be categorized into two main types: single nanomaterials and composite nanomaterials. Single nanomaterials involve uniform coatings made primarily from one type of nanoparticle, offering specific properties such as enhanced hardness or hydrophobicity. In contrast, composite nanomaterials combine multiple nanoparticles or other materials, resulting in coatings with improved performance characteristics, such as increased durability, thermal resistance, or multifunctionality. Both types are widely used in industries like electronics, automotive, and healthcare for enhancing surface properties.</p></p>
