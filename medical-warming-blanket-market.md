<p><p>The medical warming blanket market is categorized into three main types: full body, lower body, and upper body. Full body warming blankets provide comprehensive coverage, ensuring overall body warmth and comfort, often used during surgeries or in recovery rooms. Lower body warming blankets focus on heating the legs and feet, beneficial for patients with circulatory issues. Upper body warming blankets concentrate on warming the chest and arms, ideal for pre-operative care and maintaining patient temperature during procedures. Each type serves specific clinical needs.</p></p>
