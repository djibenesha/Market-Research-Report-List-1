<p><p>The canola protein market encompasses various types, primarily hydrolyzed rapeseed protein and canola isolate protein. Hydrolyzed rapeseed protein is created by breaking down protein molecules, enhancing digestibility and solubility, making it suitable for functional foods and dietary supplements. Canola isolate protein, on the other hand, undergoes a purification process to obtain a high-protein content with minimal fat and carbohydrate levels, catering to health-conscious consumers. Both types are gaining traction in food and beverage applications due to their nutritional benefits and plant-based appeal.</p></p>
