Roller conveyors utilize cylindrical rollers to transport items, ideal for heavy loads. Belt conveyors feature a continuous loop of material for smooth movement, suitable for lighter goods. Overhead conveyors maximize floor space by suspending items, commonly used in manufacturing. Pallet conveyors are designed for palletized loads, enhancing stability and control. Other types encompass specialized systems addressing unique logistical needs, ensuring versatile and streamlined operations.</p></p>
