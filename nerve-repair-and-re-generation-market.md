<p><p>The nerve repair and regeneration market focuses on therapeutics and devices aimed at restoring nerve function after injury. Key types include nerve conduits and nerve wraps. Nerve conduits are tubular structures that guide regenerating nerve fibers, providing a pathway for growth and promoting repair. Nerve wraps, on the other hand, offer protective support around damaged nerves, enhancing stability and facilitating healing. Both types play vital roles in improving patient outcomes following peripheral nerve injuries and surgical repairs.</p></p>
