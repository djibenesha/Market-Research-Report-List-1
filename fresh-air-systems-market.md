<p><p>Fresh air systems are categorized into two market types: pipe type and non-pipe type. Pipe type systems, as the name suggests, utilize ductwork to distribute fresh air throughout a building, ensuring efficient ventilation and temperature control. In contrast, non-pipe type systems rely on alternative methods, such as fans or natural ventilation, to introduce fresh air without extensive ducting. Each type offers distinct advantages, catering to various building designs and energy efficiency requirements, enhancing indoor air quality and comfort.</p></p>
